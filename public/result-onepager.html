MACOC Website Refresh — Results

Musical Arts Competition of Orange County · www.musicalartsoc.org · 2026 season fully launched

Replaced Wix + Jotform with a self-hosted Next.js site backed by Google Sheets and PayPal. Saving ~$1,500/year while unifying registration, content, and payment status into a single Google Sheet.

What changed

Before (Wix + Jotform)After
Registration UXSeparate Jotform page per division (off-site)Built into the site — consistent branding, mobile-first, division-specific flows (ensembles, multi-instrument selection, file upload)
Where data livesWix, Jotform, PayPal (3 places)Google Sheets (single source of truth)
Editing contentWix editorEdit a Google Sheet cell → site updates automatically
Design & structureGeneric Wix template; information spread across many pagesRedesigned, mobile-first site; scattered content consolidated into clear sections (About, Competition, Divisions, Winners, Directory); SEO-optimized (sitemap, JSON-LD, OG)

How it’s managed going forward

Chairs / Admins (no developer needed)
  • Edit dates, fees, contacts, winners → Google Sheets
  • See who registered → Registrations tabs in the Sheet
  • Verify payments → PayPal dashboard + Sheet’s Paid column
  • View uploaded birth certificates → Cloudinary link in each row
Developer support (occasional)
  • Adding/removing divisions or form fields
  • Opening/closing registration per division
  • Yearly season rollover and design updates

All accounts — domain, Google Sheets, PayPal, Cloudinary — are owned by MACOC. No vendor lock-in.

Recommended next steps

  • Date-based auto-close for registration (today, deadlines require a code change).
  • Email on submit in addition to email on payment, so applicants always get a receipt.
  • Optional admin dashboard for board members who prefer not to use Google Sheets directly.
